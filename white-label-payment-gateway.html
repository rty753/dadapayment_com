What Is a White-Label Payment Gateway?

Own the brand. Skip the infrastructure build.

A white-label payment gateway is a complete payment processing platform that operates under your brand identity. Every element your merchants and their customers interact with — the checkout page, merchant dashboard, email notifications, API documentation, and settlement reports — carries your company name, logo, and design.

Behind the scenes, Dada Payment provides the technology stack: the payment processing engine, server infrastructure, payment method integrations, fraud prevention systems, and compliance framework. You get the full capability of a mature payment platform without the years of development and millions in investment it would take to build one.

This model is ideal for payment service providers (PSPs), fintech startups, merchant aggregators, and enterprises that want to offer payment processing services under their own brand. You control the merchant relationships, set your own fees, and build your payment business — we provide the technology that makes it all work.

White-Label Features

Everything you need to run a branded payment gateway operation.

Custom Branding

Your logo, color scheme, typography, and visual identity applied across every touchpoint. Checkout pages, dashboards, emails, and documentation all reflect your brand. No Dada Payment branding visible to your merchants or their customers.

Your Domain

The entire gateway operates on your custom domain. Checkout at checkout.yourdomain.com, dashboard at dashboard.yourdomain.com, and API at api.yourdomain.com. SSL certificates and DNS configuration handled by our team.

Merchant Management

Onboard, manage, and configure merchants through your admin panel. Set individual payment method access, configure risk rules per merchant, manage KYC documentation, and control merchant-level settings.

Fee Control

Set your own pricing structure for merchants. Define transaction fees, monthly charges, setup fees, and any custom pricing tiers. The spread between your merchant rates and our wholesale rates is your margin. Full revenue transparency.

A third-party payment system is an external service that processes transactions between buyers and sellers without requiring the merchant to establish direct relationships with banks or payment networks.

Dada Payment acts as a third-party payment processor, handling the complexity of payment routing, currency conversion, settlement, and compliance so merchants can focus on their core business. This model reduces setup time, lowers costs, and provides access to multiple payment methods through a single integration.

Payment gateway rental allows businesses to use a fully configured payment processing infrastructure without the cost and complexity of building their own. With Dada Payment's rental model, you get access to our established gateway, merchant accounts, and payment channels on a subscription basis.

This is ideal for businesses that need to start processing payments quickly or want to test new markets before committing to a full integration. Setup can be completed within 24-48 hours. Learn more on our Payment System Rental page.

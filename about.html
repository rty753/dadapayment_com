Our Story

Dada Payment was founded in 2020 with a clear vision: to provide seamless, secure, and scalable payment gateway solutions for merchants operating in emerging Asian markets. We recognized that businesses in regions like India, Indonesia, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Vietnam, the Philippines, and Thailand faced unique challenges when it came to accepting and processing payments online.

Traditional payment providers often overlooked these markets or offered solutions that were too rigid, too expensive, or poorly adapted to local payment preferences. We set out to change that. From our earliest days, we focused on building deep integrations with local payment methods, banking networks, and mobile wallets to ensure that our merchants could reach every customer, no matter how they prefer to pay.

Since our founding, we have grown to serve over 500 merchants across seven countries, processing millions of transactions and helping businesses of all sizes unlock the potential of some of the world's fastest-growing digital economies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the payment gap in emerging markets. We believe that every merchant, whether a startup or an established enterprise, deserves access to a reliable, modern payment infrastructure that works for their specific market.

We accomplish this by combining cutting-edge technology with deep local expertise. Our team understands the regulatory landscapes, consumer behaviors, and banking systems of each market we serve. This allows us to offer solutions that are not just technically robust, but genuinely tailored to the needs of businesses operating in these regions.

Whether you need a fully managed payment gateway system, a flexible payment system rental, or a white-label gateway you can brand as your own, Dada Payment has the expertise and infrastructure to support your growth.
